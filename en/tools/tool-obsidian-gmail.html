Problem & Solution

Before

Every AI session starts from zero — re-explaining context, work style, and preferences every time. Chat outputs are disposable. Repetitive tasks still end up on your plate.

After

Your knowledge, judgment, and work patterns accumulate in Obsidian. The AI employee reads them every session and acts in your place. Instructions compound — the more you add, the closer it gets to being your alter ego.

How It Works

01 Set up an Obsidian vault as the AI employee's workspace (one-time)
02 Write your work style and decision criteria into notes — the AI interviews you and helps fill them in
03 Hand off one recurring task. Split work by role across multiple AI employees and save outputs as notes

Background

Using AI as a disposable chat tool felt like leaving most of its value on the table. Just like money compounds as an asset, AI instructions compound too — if you save them as reusable patterns instead of throwing them away. I split the roles: Obsidian as the "brain" (the knowledge base you own), and AI as the "hands" (the worker that executes). They share the same notes. You can also connect it to live context like email or calendar when needed, but the core idea is building and owning your own knowledge as a long-term asset.
