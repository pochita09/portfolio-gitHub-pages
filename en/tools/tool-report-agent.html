Problem & Solution

Before

After every site visit, workers had to review photos and manually fill in reports — often taking 10+ minutes per report.

After

Take a photo on your phone and send it to the AI. A draft report is ready in seconds — just review and send.

How It Works

01 Take a photo at the job site
02 Send the photo to the tool
03 Review the generated report and send it

Key Feature (Flexible Format)

Report formats vary by site and client — different cell positions, different fields. This tool doesn't lock you into a single template. It adapts to whatever format you're already using, placing content in the right spots even when the layout differs. New format? No rebuild needed.

Background

I heard that field workers were spending time every day filling in reports. I thought: "If you have the photo, the content can be written." People who tried it told me it dramatically cut the time they spent on reports.
