Problem & Solution

Before

Every small policy question meant digging through thick handbooks or pinging HR — pulling everyone away from real work.

After

Type your question in plain language and get a document-grounded answer instantly. If it's not in the docs, the AI says so — no misinformation risk.

How It Works

01 Load company PDF documents into the system (one-time setup)
02 Open the chat and ask your question in plain language
03 Receive a document-grounded answer. If the info isn't there, it says "Not found in the documents"

Background

At multiple companies I heard the same thing: "I never read the employee handbook — I just ask HR." The documents exist but nobody uses them. I built this to fix that. Since it works with any industry's documents, it makes a versatile demo of what RAG can do.
